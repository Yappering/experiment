2024-05_collectibles_shop_bundle

Treatment 1: Shop with bundle
This treatment, when enabled, will fetch https://canary.discord.com/api/v9/collectibles-categories?include_bundles=true and display bundles in the shop
Treatment 2: Bundle + Use Now Non-Bundle
This treatment, when enabled, when the "Use Now" button is pressed on a bundle card in the shop, the items in that bundle will be equipped on your profile
